1. Service Description

GalpiDock is a Chrome/Whale browser extension that replaces your new tab page with a customizable dashboard. It provides bookmark and web clipping management tools along with various widgets including Clock, Weather, Search, Calendar, Memo, and Quote.

2. Acceptance of Terms

3. Data We Access

GalpiDock accesses the following data to provide its functionality:

  • Google Account Information — When you optionally connect Google Calendar, we access your Google account via OAuth 2.0 to retrieve calendar events.
  • Bookmarks — Your browser bookmarks are read and managed to display them in the bookmark widget.
  • Browsing History & Top Sites — Read to display recently visited and most visited sites.
  • Widget Settings & Memo Content — User-created content such as memos, layout preferences, and widget configurations.
  • Location Data — Approximate location is used only when the weather widget is enabled.

4. How We Use Your Data

All data accessed by GalpiDock is used solely to provide the extension's features:

  • Bookmark and browsing data are used to display your personalized dashboard.
  • Google Calendar data is fetched and cached locally (up to 5 minutes) to display upcoming events.
  • Location data is sent only to OpenWeatherMap to retrieve weather information.
  • All settings and user content are stored locally in your browser via chrome.storage.local.

We do not sell, share, or transmit your personal data to any third party beyond what is described above.

5. Third-Party Services
